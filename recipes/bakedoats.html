Vegan Baked Oats

Description

Here is a delicious recipe to make vegan baked oats! Perfect treat for fall <3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Old-Fashioned Rolled Oats
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • ½ yogurt of choice
  • 1 ½ cups almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon cinnammon
  • frozen berries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Slightly oil a 9-inch x 11-inch baking pan.
  2. Peel the bananas, place them in the baking pan and mash them roughly until they are smooth and a puree-like texture forms.
  3. Add chia seeds, yogurt, cinnamon, rolled oats, almond milk, and half the amount of frozen berries.
  4. Stir gently to evenly combine all the ingredients into the baking pan.
  5. Stir gently to evenly combine all the ingredients into the baking pan.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining frozen berries on top of the pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until set but still moist in the center. You can bake it longer if you prefer a dryer oatmeal texture, it's up to you.
  8. Cool down in the pan for 20 minutes then serve lukewarm with a drizzle of maple syrup, a dust of cinnamon, or almond milk/yogurt for extra moisture
